# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Always verify the sender before clicking on any link or downloading attachments
- Check website URLs carefully (look for HTTPS and correct domain names)
- Never share passwords, OTPs, or personal details with anyone
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly

## Financial / UPI Safety
- Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps
- Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown individuals
- Double-check recipient details before sending money
- Immediately block your bank account if suspicious activity is detected

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Helpline Number: 1930 (India Cyber Crime Helpline)
