Description:

I created my digital literacy infographic using Canva, a free and user-friendly design tool. The infographic highlights key aspects of digital literacy, including its definition, essential digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and professional online presence. I structured the design using a clean layout with icons and minimal text to ensure clarity and visual appeal.

One interesting aspect of this task was learning how to present information in a concise and visually engaging manner rather than using long paragraphs. I found it slightly challenging to balance content and design, as including too much information made the infographic cluttered. However, this helped me understand the importance of simplicity and effective communication in digital design.
