Do’s:

1.Maintain a professional profile picture and bio
2.Share projects, achievements, and learning progress
3.Verify information before posting
4.Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
5.Adjust privacy settings to protect personal data


Don’ts:


1.Don’t post offensive, abusive, or controversial content
2.Don’t share personal/sensitive information publicly
3.Don’t spread misinformation or fake news
4.Don’t use inappropriate language or slang in professional spaces
5.Don’t engage in online arguments or negativity
