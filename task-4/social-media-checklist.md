## Do’s:

1.Maintain a professional profile picture and bio<br>
2.Share projects, achievements, and learning progress<br>
3.Verify information before posting<br>
4.Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely<br>
5.Adjust privacy settings to protect personal data<br>
<br>
<br>

## Don’ts:


1.Don’t post offensive, abusive, or controversial content<br>
2.Don’t share personal/sensitive information publicly<br>
3.Don’t spread misinformation or fake news<br>
4.Don’t use inappropriate language or slang in professional spaces<br>
5.Don’t engage in online arguments or negativity<br>
